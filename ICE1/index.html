Traveling

5500 Miles In One Month!

I really like to travel. This last year I took several road trips with my family. The longest one was from Ann Arbor, Michigan, into Canada through the Detroit-Windsor Tunnel. We drove along the St Lawrence Seaway through 4 provinces and then took a 6 hour ferry ride to Newfoundland. After a week of Camping in Newfoundland, we headed back through the states, this time entering through Maine. We then drove to Virginia for a week and then back home.

Half of the US and Canada In One Year!

Earlier in the year we also took a road trip along the Mississippi River and then down into central Texas. On the way back we drove along the Gulf Coast and came up through Alabama. During Spring Break, we visited Puerto Rico. Later, over Christmas/Winter break we took another road trip to Florida.
